Spagetti

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • 1 pound 93/7 beef
  • Sauce
  • vegtable oil
  • Salt
  • Seasoning Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Basil
  • Oregeno

Steps:

Pasta

  1. Fill pot with Water and add salt and vegtable oil, wait for water to boil.
  2. Once water is boiling, add pasta in and wait for pasta to cook until al dente.
  3. drain pasta using a collander.

Meat/Sauce

  1. If meat is frozen, defrost meat using microwave.
  2. Cook meat in a frying pan.
  3. Add Seasoning Salt and pepper to meat and cook meat until golden brown.
  4. remove meat and drain grease.
  5. Put meat in sauce pot and add one jar of sauce to the meat. put stove on medium heat.
  6. add oregeno, basil, salt, and pepper to taste
  7. wait for sauce to start bubbling and let combine for 5-10
